Heidi & Reed

The Post Wedding Party 2024

On 12/31/23, Reed & Heidi Meher were formally married in their home with a few family members present. To celebrate, they are having a grand post-wedding party in Heidi's hometown, Hoyt Lakes, MN.

WHEN: OCT 5, 2024 | 3PM - 10PM

WHERE: At the Hoyt Lakes Community Center in Hoyt Lakes, MN

WHAT: to bring? A bottle of champagne, if you like! Or bring a dish to share. There will be grilling, snacks, and beverages of all kinds available. Reach out to Heidi or Reed if you have questions or plan to bring a dish!

ATTIRE: Champagne Cozy (whatever the heck you like, but dressing up is fun!)

Gifts are welcome, however we are in a transitional time and have limited space for just about everything. Cash gifts and donations to party food and supplies are most welcome!
